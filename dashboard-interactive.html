Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Go to the "Book Appointment" tab, fill in your details, select your preferred date and time, and submit the form.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel your appointment from the "My Tickets" section by clicking the cancel button next to your ticket.

How does ticket swapping work?

You can request to swap your appointment time with another patient. Go to "Swap Tickets" to see available swap requests or create your own.
